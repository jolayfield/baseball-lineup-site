Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Baseball Lineup ("the App") is a youth baseball team management tool. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your email address when you create an account.
  • Team data: Team names, player names, jersey numbers, and game schedules you enter into the App.
  • Usage data: Lineups, practice plans, and scorebook entries you create.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to operate the App — to store your teams, generate lineups, and display your season stats. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted database platform. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

Children's Privacy

The App stores player names and jersey numbers for youth athletes. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Coaches and parents who enter player information are responsible for having appropriate consent. Player data is limited to first name, last name, and jersey number — no contact information, photos, or sensitive data is collected about players.

Sharing with Other Users

Team owners can invite other users (coaches, scorekeepers, viewers) to access team data via invite links or codes. You control who has access to your team.

Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, we send local timer alerts for practice block countdowns. We do not send marketing notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Data Deletion

You can delete your team data at any time within the App. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below and we will remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
